A Brief History of Minecraft Create Mod

The create mod was made in July 18th 2019, and started with just one developer, simibubi.

In The Beginning

In the beginning Create Mod was a simple hobby project . It quickly turned into more than that. It began as a forge only mod, but quickly adapted to the Fabric environment in 2022 by a separate enthusiast.

The Continued Development

The Create Mod has been non-stop developed and upkept since its first making in 2019. It has over 55 different versions for forge, as the minecraft environment adapted and the modding communities wants changed.

The Massive Spike In Popularity

Create mod has grew a massive amount since its creation, gaining more than 68 Million! downloads on the Forge Version alone! It has grown to have over 150 patreon supporters!

What Is The Create Mod?

Create is a minecraft mod created by simibubi that allows you to make Contraptions,
and automate processes using Rotational Power/Stress Units.
It adds many mechanical components and block variants to the game.
Create is also a decoration mod, adding over 550+ Blocks!

The Reason For Its Popularity

The Create Mod is liked as the added elements of tech are designed to
